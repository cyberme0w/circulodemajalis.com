CIRCULODEMAJALIS

Association for Citizenship through Art, Tradition, and Knowledge

We are a group of citizens from the village of Canha and beyond, who decided to use art as a tool to educate, unite, and help the citizens of Canha and its surroundings to actively participate and thus develop the village of Canha. We are a non-profit association.
