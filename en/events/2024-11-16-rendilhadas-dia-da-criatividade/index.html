Rendilhadas / Creativity Day 2024

16 November 2024

MAJALIS celebrates Creativity Day annually, recognizing that each person’s creativity is the surest tool for adapting to social changes and facing the challenges of new times, ensuring a life of quality.

This principle is one of the pillars of our work, which we wish to continue to develop.

The Challenge to the Community

This year, we launched a challenge to the community: to reuse unused lace and, with it, build a tribute to women. Women, whom we recognize as the main workers in this type of work, were the central focus of this initiative.

The project involved the collaboration of senior and junior users of Santa Casa da Misericórdia and Casa do Povo de Canha. It was a joint effort, carried out with commitment and sharing.

Opening of the Exhibition

We inaugurated the exhibition with the participation of those who contributed to the project and with the presence of the community who were able and willing to collaborate.

The moment was celebrated with a gathering around roasted chestnuts and água-pé, kindly provided by the Canha Parish Council.
