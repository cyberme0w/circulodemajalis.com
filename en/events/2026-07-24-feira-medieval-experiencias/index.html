Immersive Game: “The Witch of Canha”

In the 14th century, a Witch condemned to death cast a curse upon the entire village of Canha… and now she has returned to claim the souls of her descendants.

Enter her ancient home, confront her servants, uncover her secrets, and try to stop the ritual that could condemn the entire village.

A unique experience that combines immersive theater, escape room, horror passage, and live-action gameplay.

But beware…

You will only have 3 lives to survive the Witch of Canha.

  • Requires advance ticket purchase (15 moneys)
  • It can be purchased as a package with “Moonlight Dinners”.
