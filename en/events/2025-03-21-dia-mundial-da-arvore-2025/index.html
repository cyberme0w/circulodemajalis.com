World Tree Day 2025

21 March 2025

On March 21st, at the invitation of the CIRCULODEMAJALIS Association and jointly organized with the Canha Ethnographic Museum, magic happened!

With the participation of associations, groups, families, communities, residents of Canha, and others, the collective exhibition of the Enchanted Forest (Floresta Encantada) delighted young and old alike, drawing attention to the importance of forest preservation. We all have art within us, let’s use it to change the world!

Well done!

Schedule

4:00 p.m. - Planting of plant species in the vertical garden with the help of Catl. and seniors from Casa do Povo, Canha Preschool, and Catl. and seniors from Sta. Casa da Misericórdia. Location: Rua do Lagar in Canha.

Around 4:30 p.m. - Opening of the exhibition “The Enchanted Forest” with poetry recitation followed by a social gathering. Location: Ethnographic Museum of Canha
