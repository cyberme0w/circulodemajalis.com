Book Launch "A voz dos montes" by Gabriel Mendes

26 September 2025

On September 26, at 5:00 p.m. at the Chapel of Santo António at the Pátio d’Água in Montijo, come to the launch of the book by Gabriel Mendes, one of our members at Majalis.

With the “One Year, One Book” project, we affirm our willingness to also support the members of our Association! In addition, the donation for each book will go towards anthropological studies in Canha!

Support this cause! Come along, spread the word, and join us at the beautiful and restored Chapel! We will have a get-together, muscatel, and the book will be presented by the writer and director Domingos Lobo!

With the support of the Câmara Municipal of Montijo.
