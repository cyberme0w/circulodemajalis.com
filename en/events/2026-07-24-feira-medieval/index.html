Canha Medieval Fair 2026

24-26 July 2026

On July 24th, 25th, and 26th, 2026, Canha travels back to the 13th century to welcome the Royal couple D. Dinis and D. Isabel of Aragon, Queen St. Isabel.

Three unmissable days at one of the region’s biggest events!

Concerts, medieval tournaments, dances and theatre, merchants and artisans, exhibitions and immersive experiences, a “Souk”, and much more…!

This is a journey into our history, to the reign that prepared Portugal for the great Age of Discoveries.

Come travel through time, experience history and have fun at the Canha Medieval Fair.

Canha Medieval Fair Schedule:

  • Friday, 24th: 3 PM to 2 AM
  • Saturday, 25th: 2 PM to 2 AM
  • Sunday, 26th: 12 PM to 10 PM
