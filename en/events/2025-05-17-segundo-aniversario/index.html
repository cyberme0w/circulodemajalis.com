IIⁿᵈ Majalis Anniversary

17 May 2025

On May 17th, we celebrated our Majalis Anniversary.

Watch the video of the event here:

Click to open on YouTube

The gathering took place at the Canha Volunteer Fire Department with the show “A Lily in the Heart”. The room was too small for so many people, which made us very happy and grateful. We started with the Cavaquinhos Sinfonias Group and the Serenatas e Sinfonias ao Luar Group, followed by a performance by the Pegões and Canha Senior Academy. The children of the Banda da Casa do Povo de Canha showed off their musical skills, and Cláudia and Manuel Veríssimo treated us, as usual, to their beautiful voices.

There was a poem recited by Maria do Rosário, and Joana Maia’s Hip Hop dance group left everyone speechless, young and old alike, with their rhythm and excellent choreography. The Sevillanas La Caña and the Folkloric and Ethnographic Ranch of Santa Casa da Misericórdia, who have already accustomed us to their quality, were also there and were greatly applauded!

Finally, our cake, for which some members of CIRCULODEMAJALIS rushed to provide the ingredients, was deliciously prepared under the guidance of Rosa Oliveira and baked at Santa Casa.

A special thanks to all the participants and helpers who made this wonderful day possible!
