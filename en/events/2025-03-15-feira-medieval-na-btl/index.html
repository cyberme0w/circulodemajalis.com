Canha Medieval Fair '25 at BTL

15 March 2025

At 18:30 on Saturday, March 15, 2025, we were at the BTL, at FIL in Lisbon, which is the country’s largest tourism fair, promoting the Canha Medieval Fair, together with the Montijo City Council. There was live music, tastings and lots of entertainment. Our thanks to the Montijo City Council and especially to everyone who came to visit us!
