Iˢᵗ Majalis Anniversary

01 June 2023

Our anniversary is celebrated in May, on a date to be determined.

And it is in May because the lily of the valley (our Majalis) is the flower of May. However, this first anniversary was celebrated on June 1.

We brought our members together with the community who were able and willing to attend. At the Canha Fire Station, there were:

  • The Sevillanas of Canha: La Canha
  • The Ethnographic Ranch of Canha
  • The children of the SCM of Canha’s CATL
  • The Academy of Seniors of Canha and Pegões

These entities performed after the dramatization of the legend of the nightingale and the lily of the valley. The presentation featured performances by Cláudia Carvalheira (who also sang), Tiago Almeida, Diana Almeida, as well as the younger children from catechism class.

Then we sang Happy Birthday!
