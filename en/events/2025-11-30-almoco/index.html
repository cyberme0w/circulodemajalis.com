Lunch gathering at Moinho Novo 2025

30 November 2025

On November 30, we held our second MAJALIS lunch gathering at the beautiful Herdade do Moinho Novo, which our member José Carlos kindly made available free of charge.

It was the day after Creativity Day, so we visited the “Despertares” exhibition with members who were unable to attend the opening.

Three groups that helped us with the Medieval Fair were also invited to the lunch gathering, and we had another opportunity to thank them for their commitment and great help. We also distributed individual participation certificates and those from partner organizations.
