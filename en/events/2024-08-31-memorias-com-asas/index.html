Exposition / Book Launch "Memórias com Asas"

31 August 2024

In September 2024, we held the presentation of the book “Memórias com Asas” (Memories with Wings) and the exhibition “Aves em Canha” (Birds in Canha) at our headquarters, which was still undergoing the necessary renovations.

This book proposes a rural and an urban walking trail, through which people can discover the birds of the region.

Studies, interviews, illustrations, photographs, observation trails, and the graphic presentation of the book were all prepared by some of our members.

The illustrations in the book were done by Cristina Archer and the originals were used to create the exhibition.

Two wine tastings took place in parallel:

  • one by the Stanley Ho Foundation, with wines produced in the Canha floodplains
  • another of organic wines from Herdade do Alto do Pina, in the parish of Poceirão.
