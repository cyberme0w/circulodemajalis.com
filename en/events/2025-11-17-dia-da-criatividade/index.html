Creativity Day 2025: "Despertares"

17 November 2025

Monday, November 17, at 4:30 p.m., at the Canha Ethnographic Museum, the collective exhibition “Despertares” (Awakening) will open. It will be on display until the end of December during museum hours. Come visit us!

With the theme “Despertares,” MAJALIS invites everyone (friends, associations, individuals) to participate with a creative work using mostly recycled materials (paper, newspaper, fabrics, lace, plastics, wood, cork, etc.) and you can use any techniques you wish (collages, modeling, painting, etc.).
