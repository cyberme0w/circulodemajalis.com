Canha Medieval Fair '26 at the BTL

28 February 2026

On the 28th, we were honored to welcome the president of the Lisbon Regional Tourism Authority, Dr. Carla Salsinha, and the councilman of the Montijo City Council, Ilídio Massacote.

We would like to extend a special thank you to the Lisbon Regional Tourism Authority (ERTL), which supported us throughout the event, played an essential role in our presentation, and also confirmed ERTL’s support for this year’s event!

We would also like to thank the Montijo Municipal Council for their presence and for giving us a prominent place in their program for BTL 2026.

Finally, we would like to thank the Canha Parish Council for their presence and support during our presentation.

A big thank you to everyone who attended.

We hope you enjoyed our participation in BTL 2026. We will be revealing a number of surprises soon.

We look forward to seeing you at the Canha Medieval Fair ‘26.
