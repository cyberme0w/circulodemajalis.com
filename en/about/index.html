About CIRCULODEMAJALIS

Founded January 13th 2023, the non-profit association CIRCULODEMAJALIS aims to:

  • develop solidarity and social responsibility among citizens,
  • value creativity and culture as pillars of freedom and drivers of change, and
  • build bridges between communities and other associations.

Among other activities, these are part of our action plan:

  • Workshops
  • Reuse of materials (World Tree Day, Rendilhadas)
  • Art galleries (Aves em Canha, Rendilhadas)
  • Recreation of historic events (2025 Medieval Fair)
  • Create conditions for community participation by all residents
  • Create activities that recreate traditional events: bread making, lace and embroidery, gardening, traditional crafts, pottery, etc. (Rendilhadas)
  • Collaboration in decorating the streets for the Canha Festivities
  • Book publications (Memórias com Asas)
  • Creation of an annual event focusing on music and art in May
  • Study of streets
  • Create a public meeting place where art is present
  • Complete the construction work at headquarters
