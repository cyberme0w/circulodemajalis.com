Arbor Day 2026

Published: January 17, 2026

On the 20th of March we will celebrate Arbor Day!

Join us at the Ethnographic Museum of Canha at 16:00 and come and see what we have prepared for you.

We will inaugurate an exhibition in which you can participate:

We provide the base: a pallet (which can be purchased at the Canha Museum). The trees must be made in 3D, with a minimum height of 150 cm, with roots and recycled or natural materials, but they must be waterproof as the exhibition will be held outdoors. Entries must be submitted by March 16th at the Canha Museum.

In addition to the palette, we can provide some basic materials (such as glue, paint, etc.) upon request via private message.

This event will be supported by the Canha Parish Council.
