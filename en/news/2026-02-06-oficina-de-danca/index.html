Register now for the "Oficinas de Dança" (dance workshop)

Published: February 6, 2026

Registration for the “Oficinas de Dança” dance workshop is now open!

In these dance workshops, you will have the opportunity to learn Medieval Dances from various origins (Italian, French, German) to participate in the Medieval Fair and other community events.

Two groups will be formed, one for adults and one for young people, with written authorization required from parents or guardians for those under 16 years of age.

How to Register

You can register by email: circulodemajalis@gmail.com, with the Registration Form filled.

You may ask for additional information by phone at +351 934441630 , or +351 962150073 .

Information

  • Start date: March 2026
  • Cost per person: Between €10 and €20 (the price depends on the number of registrations)
  • Location: Canha (Volunteer Fire Department)
  • Duration: 2 hours every 15 days

Classes will be taught by instructors Ana Costa and Beatriz Boleto.
