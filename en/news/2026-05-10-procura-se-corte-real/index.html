Royal Court Wanted for a Day at the 2026 Canha Medieval Fair

Published: May 10, 2026

The Canha Medieval Fair presents a unique opportunity! Gather a group of 6 friends (6 people in total, comprising ladies-in-waiting and pages) and participate in the Medieval Fair as members of the Royal Court. Each court will serve as historical reenactors for one of the days of the Medieval Fair, accompanying the Kings and Queens in the Royal Procession and at the Tournaments. To register, please send us a message listing the names of your group members and the specific day(s) on which you would like to participate.

The prize for each member of the selected courts is a meal for one person at Omaggio - Praia do Sal in Alcochete.

The meal includes food and either a non-alcoholic beverage or Dona Joaquina organic wine (ages 18+ only).

Registration is via private message to the Canha Medieval Fair Facebook page, including the full names of all group members and the group’s availability.
