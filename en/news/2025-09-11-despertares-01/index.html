Looking for artists for Creativity Day 2025

Published: September 11, 2025
Event: Creativity Day 2025: "Despertares"

With the theme “Despertares” (Awakenings), MAJALIS invites everyone (friends, associations, individuals) to participate with a creative work using mostly recycled materials (paper, newspaper, fabrics, lace, plastics, wood, cork, etc.) using any techniques you wish (collages, modeling, painting, etc.).

Please inform the Association of the basis on which you will be creating your work and whether you need support. We need to know who is participating by November 2.

The deadline for submission is November 12, 2025!
