Volunteer Registration Open for the 2026 Canha Medieval Fair

Published: May 12, 2026

Volunteer registration is open for the 2026 Canha Medieval Fair, and you can register now by filling out the form at the link below.

We are looking for volunteers for:

  • Back Office: supporting the Fair’s organizing members, performing tasks related to its smooth operation, among others (similar);
  • Visitor Support: at the information booth, guiding and providing information when requested; directing and managing entry to the moonlit dinners, the colloquium, and the theater;
  • Artist Support: as a guide during the Fair, proficiency in English (required), Italian and/or French (preferred).
