Come Join the Façade Contest at the 2026 Canha Medieval Fair

Published: May 20, 2026

Do you live in Canha?

Registration is now open for the “Best Façade” contest! 🏆

You can use straw, wood, cardboard, fabrics, burlap, create banners to hang in the windows, decorate with earthenware items, place rustic stools by the door - let your imagination run wild!

The prizes (generously provided by the Canha Parish Council) are very enticing!

  • 1st Place 🥇: A lamb
  • 2nd Place 🥈: A pig
  • 3rd Place 🥉: Two chickens

To register, simply send an email to: feiramedievaldecanha@gmail.com, including your full name, full address, and contact phone number.

You have nothing to lose, and you’ll help beautify our village during the days of the 2026 Canha Medieval Fair! We look forward to seeing you there!
