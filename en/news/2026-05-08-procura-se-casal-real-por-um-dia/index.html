Royal Couple Wanted for a Day at the 2026 Canha Medieval Fair

Published: May 8, 2026

Are you ready to be part of the Canha Medieval Fair? We have a unique opportunity for you! We are looking for Kings and Queens to join the Medieval Fair as historical reenactors. Each King and Queen will appear during one of the days of the Medieval Fair, participating in the Royal Procession, the Tournaments, and the Moonlight Banquets (free admission for the King and Queen to the Moonlight Banquets on the day they are performing). To apply, please send us a private message via the Canha Medieval Fair Facebook page.
