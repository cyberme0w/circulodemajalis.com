Christmas Market of Pegões

Published: January 4, 2026

We would like to thank Associação Arte com Vida for the opportunity to participate in the Pegões Christmas Market, which took place on December 12, 13, and 14, 2025, at the Cruzamento de Pegões church sports center, where we showcased some of the work created during 2025 by the participants of our association.

The party was very well organized and wonderful! Thank you.
