Register now for the "Teatro Raíz de Canha" group!

Published: January 17, 2026

Registration is now open for the “Teatro Raíz de Canha” group.

If you like creating characters, improvising, and participating in small off-stage animations in the future, sign up! Two groups will be formed, one for adults and one for young people. Written authorization from parents or guardians is required for children aged 16 and under.

How to register

You can register by email: circulodemajalis@gmail.com,

or by phone at: +351 934441630 , ou +351 962150073 .

Details

  • Start date: March 2026
  • Cost per person: Between €10 and €20 (the price depends on the number of registrations)
  • Location: Canha (Volunteer Fire Department)
  • Duration: 2 hours every 15 days

What to expect from the course

  • Theater training focused on improvisation techniques and interaction in an off-stage context.
  • Inclusion in the training of historical aspects relevant to the construction of characters and the interactions between characters and the historical environment.
  • Possibility of performing during the Canha Medieval Fair 2026 and/or in activities prior to the Fair.

Classes will be taught by accredited trainer Dinis Binnema (already known to everyone for his performances at the 2025 Canha Medieval Fair).
