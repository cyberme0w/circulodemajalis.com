Register now for the "Teatro Raíz de Canha" group!

Published: January 17, 2026

You can now register for the “Teatro Raíz de Canha” group.

If you like creating characters, improvising, and participating in small off-stage animations in the future, sign up! There will be two groups, one for adults and one for younger people.

Classes will be taught by the accredited trainer Dinis Binnema (already known to everyone for his performances at the Canha Medieval Fair 2025).

We look forward to seeing you there!
